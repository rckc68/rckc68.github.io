Digital Image Project

I used GIMP to create the three images below. For the banner I first cropped, cut, and pasted the picture of the children holding hands into a new layer. Then crop, cut, paste the picture of the girl holding a dandelion on a new layer below the first and apply a gradient transparency to it. Then crop and resize the background picture. And finally add the text. For the icon I used the picture of the girl holding a dandelion. I scaled it down and then I applied a filter to it called cartoon. The last image was created with pictures from my vacation last year. I had to use new techniques in creating this so in addition to using the crop tool and the gradient tool, I used the brightness-contrast tool and the curves tool. For a more in depth explanation of how the banner and the icon were created, read the PDF below.

Digital Video Project

I used Windows Movie Maker to create video below. The video clips were recorded using my iPhone. I reused the background music track that I created for the audio project. I imported the video clips and picture into Windows Movie Maker, and then I created an opening title screen and added text. I placed the image after the title screen and set it to stay for 3 seconds. The first video clip plays after that and then there is a cut transition. The second video clip plays and then there is a crossfade transition. The third video clip plays and then there is a blur-through black transition between that and the ending credits. I muted the audio on all of the video clips and added in the background music track created in GarageBand. For a more in depth explanation of how the video was created, read the PDF below.
